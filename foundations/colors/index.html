Colors

GO1D uses colors purposefully to communicate how things function in the interface. This helps us create visual patterns that can make interacting with our product easier and more predictable for users.

Principles

  • Throughout the interface, GO1D uses colour meaningfully
  • In copy, use colours that ensure accessibility and legibility
